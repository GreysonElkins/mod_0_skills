class: Chair

attributes:

height (float)
material (array of strings)
isWobbly (boolean)
legCount (integer)
isEmpty (boolean)

methods:

seatCustomer (someone sits in the chair, changes a chairs empty value to false)

reupholster (the chair is made to look new, changes or adds to the material)

shimmyLeg (places a small peice of wood under one of the legs, changes wobbly to false)

addBoosterSeat (adds a boosterSeat to the chair, increasing its height)

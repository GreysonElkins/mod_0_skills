class: Chair

attributes:
height (float)
material (string)
wobbly (boolean)
leg (integer)
empty (boolean)

methods:

seatCustomer (someone sits in the chair, changes a chairs empty value to false)

reupholster (the chair is made to look new, changes the material)

shimmy (places a small peice of wood under one of the legs, changes wobbly to false)

boosterSeat (adds a boosterSeat to the chair, increasing its height)
